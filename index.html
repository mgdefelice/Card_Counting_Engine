What is Card Counting?

Card counting is a mathematical strategy used by players in blackjack to give an advantage over the dealer or house. This strategy, often called the Hi-low method, is a running tally, assigning plus 1 value to low cards and neg 1 value to high cards. When the running tally becomes greater than 5 and when following the BlackJack Strategy Chart , the player then has a higher probability to win over the house.
